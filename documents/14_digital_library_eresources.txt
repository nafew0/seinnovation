BdREN Digital Library and E-Resources

Through national-level subscriptions and partnerships, BdREN provides member
institutions with access to e-journals and digital library resources. Members
can reach licensed e-resources and e-book collections, and BdREN has arranged
access to platforms such as JSTOR for connected universities.

This e-resource access complements BdREN's connectivity, eduroam, video
conferencing and data-centre services, giving students and researchers a single
gateway to scholarly content alongside the network itself.
