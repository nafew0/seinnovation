BdREN Data Centre, Cloud and HPC

BdREN's High Performance Computing (HPC) system was implemented to provide cloud
services to all BdREN members, including higher-education institutes, medical
colleges and research institutes. The platform offers Infrastructure as a
Service (IaaS) and may be extended to Platform as a Service (PaaS) and Software
as a Service (SaaS).

Two data centres, hosted at UGC and BUET, run with redundant systems and
disaster-recovery capability. They provide floor space, power, HVAC, structured
cabling, network-switch connections and WAN links that support the virtualised
infrastructure, as well as colocation and GPU resources for member institutions.
