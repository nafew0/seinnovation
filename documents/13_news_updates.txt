BdREN News and Updates

Recent BdREN activities include participation as a sponsor at PECCII 2026, a
Generative AI training workshop held with UGC, and a workshop on AI and
institutional collaboration. BdREN also hosted a "Meet & Greet 2026" service
awareness event and expanded the high-speed campus network at the University of
Chittagong's new Arts Building.

On digital content, BdREN partnered with the University of Information Technology
and Sciences (UITS) for JSTOR access and signed a Memorandum of Understanding
with the University of Dhaka to strengthen access to e-resources. The 59th
meeting of the Board of Trustees was held in May under a new Chairperson.
