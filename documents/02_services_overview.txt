BdREN Services

BdREN delivers a broad catalogue of digital services to its connected member
institutions, spanning connectivity, collaboration, computing and content.

Its main services include high-speed Internet and data connectivity over the
national fibre-optic backbone; eduroam secure Wi-Fi roaming; video conferencing
and Zoom-based virtual meetings; data centre, cloud and high-performance
computing facilities; and access to e-journals and digital library resources
through national subscriptions.

The wider service catalogue also lists VPN and DNS, bulk emailing, learning
management (LMS), virtual desktop infrastructure (VDI), cloud storage, smart
classrooms and distance-learning theatres, software development and basic ERP
modules, training and workshops, consultancy, and grants and financial
assistance.

These services are intended to support teaching, learning, research and
administration across the member community.
