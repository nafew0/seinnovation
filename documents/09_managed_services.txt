BdREN Managed Services

BdREN is ready to provide managed services for its member organisations. Such
services range from monitoring a member institution's IT network to providing
full-service hardware, software, design, monitoring and operations.

The managed services on offer include 7x24 NOC service, maintenance services,
campus network management, data centre management, virtual conferencing, data
backup services, email management and migration, web hosting, eduroam services,
identity and access management (IAM), and email spam and phishing mitigation.

Because the scope and scale of such services can vary, the rate for managed
services is fixed through bilateral negotiation between BdREN and the member
institution.
