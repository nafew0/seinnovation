Applying for BdREN Membership

Institutions apply to BdREN through an online membership application. The
applicant selects a member category — Full, Associate, Affiliate or Industry —
and provides the institution's name and address.

The application form collects three contact roles: an Administrative contact, a
Technical contact and a Billing contact, each with a name, designation,
department, address, phone and email. Applicants indicate who is completing the
form, and may note whether the Technical and Billing contacts are the same as
the Administrative contact. Once submitted, the application is reviewed before
the connection is arranged.
