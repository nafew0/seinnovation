BdREN Membership and Connectivity

Membership of BdREN is organised into categories. Public, private and
international universities approved by the Government of Bangladesh are eligible
to become Full Members. Recognised research organisations, public or private
medical and dental colleges, and research bodies that collaborate with local or
foreign universities are eligible as Associate Members. Organisations involved in
the development and financing of the education and research sector may join as
Affiliate Members, and industry partners as Industry Members.

In short, the institutions eligible to connect to the BdREN network are public
and private universities, international universities, and recognised research
institutions and medical colleges in Bangladesh.

Membership is subject to an annual membership fee that is set by category:
25,000 BDT per year for a Full Member, 20,000 BDT per year for an Associate
Member, 15,000 BDT per year for an Affiliate Member, and 200,000 BDT per year for
an Industry Member.

Once an institution's application has been reviewed and approved, BdREN arranges
the physical fibre connection to the campus and configures access to network
services such as eduroam, video conferencing and digital library resources.
