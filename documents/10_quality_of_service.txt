BdREN Quality of Service

BdREN commits to defined service-quality targets across its network. The Dhaka
fibre ring carries a 99.75% uptime commitment, with at most 120 minutes of
downtime per month. Average packet loss is held below 0.5% on a day's average.

Latency targets include under 15 ms to peering routers and under 70 ms to
servers in Singapore, while overall network jitter to anywhere in the world is
kept below 50 ms. These commitments underpin the connectivity and collaboration
services used by member institutions every day.
