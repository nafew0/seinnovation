The BdREN Team

BdREN's work is delivered by a professional team led by its executive officers:
Mohammad Tawrit (Chief Executive Officer), Dr. Mohammad Farooq Ali Tarafder
(Chief Finance Officer) and Ekhlas Uddin Ahmed (Chief Marketing Officer).

Operations and services are organised into units covering core network, systems
and cloud, innovation, software development and sales, supported by senior and
mid-level engineers in transmission, network operations and power
infrastructure, alongside finance, accounts and administrative staff. As the
team's motto puts it, the strength of the team is each individual member, and
the strength of each member is the team.
