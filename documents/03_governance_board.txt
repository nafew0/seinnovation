BdREN Governance and Board of Trustees

BdREN is governed as a non-profit Trust by an independent Board of Trustees made
up of representatives from the universities and user communities, the University
Grants Commission (UGC), the Ministry of Education (MoE) and other members.

The Board is chaired by the Chairman of UGC. At present Professor Dr. Mamun
Ahmed, Chairman of the University Grants Commission of Bangladesh, serves as
Chairperson of the BdREN Trust, with Professor Dr. Md. Saidur Rahman as
Vice-Chairperson.

Trustees include Vice-Chancellors of member universities — among them Noakhali
Science and Technology University, Rajshahi University of Engineering &
Technology, Khulna University of Engineering & Technology and the University of
Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB) — together with representatives of the Secondary
& Higher Education Division of the Ministry of Education. The 59th meeting of the
Board of Trustees was held recently under a new Chairperson.
