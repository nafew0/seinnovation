BdREN Video Conferencing and Zoom

BdREN offers two types of video conferencing solution: physical appearance among
distant sites, and virtual appearance among remote sites. Physical meeting rooms
equipped with state-of-the-art technology have been established at almost all
public universities, 12 medical colleges, 10 research institutes and 3 private
universities.

Virtual meetings use the Zoom application, and all BdREN members are entitled to
use the Zoom facility free of cost. The platform supports online classes,
seminars, thesis defences and inter-campus meetings, including Distance Learning
and Education (DLE) courses and Digital Seminars (DigiNars) on topics such as
cyber security, ethical hacking and the financial sustainability of NRENs.
